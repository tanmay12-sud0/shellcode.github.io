What is Nearbii

Nearbii is owned by PROPE NETWORK LLP founded in July 2022 • Nearbii a local search engine which bridge the gap between service/business providers to service seekers. • We here at Nearbii have built a digital platform for those who find it difficult to scale up their services/businesses & reach masses to benefit more financially. • We offer totally free to use platform for user. • A platform where one can find any user or individual can find any service, business, individual freelancer etc a just a click in this chaotic & busy lifestyle • A boost to those who seek growth & scaling up to their services or skills & reach wider audience • Exposure for all the ambitious people out there who seek expansion of their business but lack in resources that are required for growth. • We have authentication and verifications for all the vendor who register with us so we protect our users from the fraudsters. • We have worked hard for the privacy we bring to our vendors.

Our Vision

Create a ecosystem that accommodates each and every individual on the platform to maximize their opportunities to reach large number of audience which will enhance their chances of earning. • Removing the concept of middle commission so the service provider receive the entire benefit and can offer services at lower prices so that consumers also receive the privilege to avail the services in low costs than available on any other digital platforms. Which calls for a win-win for costumers and service providers. • Offer platform at minimal fee so that we can accommodate each and every individual on our platform.
